How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Pulse account at any time. We offer two methods to delete your account:

Method 1: Delete from the App

Open the Pulse app → Go to Settings → Tap "Delete Profile" → Confirm deletion

Method 2: Request via Email

Send an email to us with your account email address requesting account deletion

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

When you delete your account, we will:

  • Permanently delete your profile information (name, email, phone number, preferences)
  • Remove all your bookmarked articles
  • Delete your account data from our servers
  • Remove your authentication credentials

Note: Some data may be retained for legal or regulatory purposes as required by law. This includes transaction records and data required for compliance.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.
